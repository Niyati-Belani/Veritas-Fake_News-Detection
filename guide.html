VERITAS FIELD GUIDE

Welcome to VERITAS — your intelligent misinformation detection assistant. Whether you're verifying breaking news, checking suspicious articles, or scanning uploaded documents, VERITAS helps you uncover hidden manipulation patterns using advanced AI-powered analysis. Simply paste text, upload a document, or provide a news URL, and let the system investigate the credibility behind the content in seconds.

🌐

Smart Text & URL Investigation

Have a suspicious headline or viral article? Paste the content directly into the Scanner or provide a live website link. VERITAS automatically extracts the important article content, ignores unnecessary clutter, and analyzes the writing style, emotional tone, and linguistic patterns to identify possible misinformation.

📄

Document Threat Analysis

Upload PDF or TXT files for deep offline analysis. The platform intelligently extracts text from your documents, cleans unwanted noise, and prepares optimized AI-readable data for accurate fake news detection — even from lengthy reports or articles.

📊

Explainable AI Insights

VERITAS doesn’t just give predictions — it explains why the content appears suspicious. The system highlights emotionally manipulative phrases, misleading wording patterns, and influential keywords that contribute to the final AI decision, making the analysis transparent and easier to trust.

How VERITAS Works

Step 01 — Content Submission

Start by submitting article text, a news website URL, or uploading a PDF/TXT document through the scanner interface.

Step 02 — Intelligent Preprocessing

The AI engine cleans and organizes the content, removes unnecessary noise, tokenizes important words, and prepares structured input for deep language analysis.

Step 03 — AI Credibility Detection

DistilBERT-powered NLP models examine semantic meaning, emotional intensity, writing behavior, and suspicious linguistic signals to estimate whether the information is real or fake.

Step 04 — Interactive Results & Insights

VERITAS generates a detailed prediction report with confidence scores, explainable AI indicators, visual analytics, and keyword-level insights so users can better understand the reasoning behind the prediction.
